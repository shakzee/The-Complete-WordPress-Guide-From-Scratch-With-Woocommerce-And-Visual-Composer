=== Description ===
Verso Lite is a responsive and mobile friendly wordpress theme specially designed for institutional, educational and corporate websites. This theme unveils your website in the efficient way on desktops, laptops, smartphones, as well as other mobile devices. This wordPress theme has an elegant design with convincing features. showcase your portfolio, sell books or offer online courses- Verso Lite lets you accomplish-it effectively. Verso Lite WordPress Theme would be the right choice for small business or an institution with an intent to promote products or services online. With the customizable features and fresh design, you can attract the visitors in a jiffy. It is completely SEO Optimized and comes with Google Font and Font Awesome support. Verso Lite Theme looks impressive on all browsers as it boasts cross browser compatibility. The best part about this theme is WooCommerce Integration support. It lets you to sell Products or Services on your website itself. Launch your institutional Website with the Verso Lite WordPress theme! We have a round the clock customer support to assist the users via email and chat. For demo, visit: http://demo.themeparrot.com/twp_verso/

=== Installation ===
1. In your admin panel, go to Appearance -> Themes and click the 'Add New' button.
2. Type in Verso Lite in the search form and press the 'Enter' key on your keyboard.
3. Click on the 'Activate' button to use your new theme right away.
4. Navigate to Appearance > Customize in your admin panel and customize to taste.
